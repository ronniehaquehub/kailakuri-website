Our Projects

Mother and Child Healthcare

KHCP provides essential maternal and child health services, including antenatal care, nutrition support, and immunisations. Notably, diseases such as measles are rarely seen due to 100% immunisation coverage in the community.

Diabetes Programme

KHCP runs a significant primary healthcare diabetes programme for the poor. This initiative offers subsidised treatment and supervision, particularly for Type 1 diabetes patients. Patients under 21 are linked to the BIRDEM-Novo Nordisk “Changing Diabetes in Children” programme, which provides free insulin.

Tuberculosis (TB) Programme

KHCP's TB programme is implemented under the Damien Foundation and is part of the government's national TB programme. As a sub-centre of the Madhupur TB clinic, KHCP provides treatment for various forms of TB, including multi-drug-resistant, child, sputum-negative, and extra-pulmonary TB cases.

General Health Services

KHCP addresses a range of health conditions prevalent in the community, such as malnutrition, ulcers, asthma, epilepsy, skin infections, anaemia, respiratory infections, and burns. The project collaborates with government agencies and other NGOs to enhance the effectiveness of these services.
